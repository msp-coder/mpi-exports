we are

The Parent Company of the Group, M/s P.SOOD & COMPANY was established in the year 1953 in Kolkata. Now the group comprises of 3 manufacturing units – two in Chennai & one in Pune, with marketing offices in Kolkata & Mumbai.

What we do

Just-in-time deliveries, assured quality and quantities with the right pricing and instant response have made us the first choice among customers.

Our Vision

We will work together to be a world renowned company, growing on a strong base of satisfied customers. Our customers will be our partners.

Our Mission

Focusing on continuous improvement, we will develop and innovate process improvements, invest in new technologies.
